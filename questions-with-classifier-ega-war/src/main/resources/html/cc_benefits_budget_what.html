With straight (also called revolving credit) you can choose to repay the full purchase amount or the minimum percentage on the repayment date. Straight purchases may have an interest free period of up to 55 days.Budget transactions allow you the flexibility and control to repay larger transactions over an extended period (e.g. 24 months) in equal instalments to suit your needs and affordability.Budget transactions are interest bearing and do not qualify for the interest free period.
