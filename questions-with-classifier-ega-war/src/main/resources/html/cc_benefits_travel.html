Travel insurance is free and automatic cover for travellers under 75 years of age who purchase and pay for the full cost of their air tickets with their Capitec Bank credit card It is valid for trips of up to 90 days. Travel insurance is intended to cover unexpected medical expenses, trip cancellation, lost luggage, flight accidents and other losses incurred while traveling, either internationally or locally.
