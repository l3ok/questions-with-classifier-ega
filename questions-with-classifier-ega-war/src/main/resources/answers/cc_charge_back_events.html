You have chargeback protection which covers you in certain instances for purchases made with your credit card. The chargeback protection is only applicable in cases where the dispute could not be resolved between you and the merchant. You may be protected in the following cases:

  • the company you purchased from went into liquidation.
  • the goods were not as described or were defective.
  • the goods were not received as promised.
  • a processing error by the bank occurred.
  • when charged multiple times or being charged for the incorrect amount.
  • you were the victim of fraud and did not authorise the purchase.
