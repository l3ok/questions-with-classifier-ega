• Log onto Selfservice.dstv.com using your Connect ID
  • Once on the My DStv Home page,
  • Click "Transfer Between Accounts" on the left Mega Nav to access the option
  • Select account you wish to transfer From, from the drop down option
  • Select account you wish to transfer To, from the drop down option
  • Enter the total amount you wish to transfer, without cents (amount up to max credit available on the From account)
  • Click the "Transfer" to submit the request.
