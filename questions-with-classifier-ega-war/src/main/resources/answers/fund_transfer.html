When a customer has two accounts (boxoffice account and residential account) they are able to move the credit they have from their residential account to the boxoffice account to give them access to use the funds available for the account with no funds.

  • Log onto Selfservice.dstv.com using your Connect ID
  • Once on the My DStv Home page,
  • Click "Balances and Payments" on the left Mega Nav and click on "Transfer between accounts"
  • Select the account to transfer from
  • Select the account too transfer to
  • Input the required amount to transfer
  • Click "Transfer" to complete the transaction
