Switching is done when you want to disconnect your home decoder and activate the holiday decoder without having to pay different subscription (you just move the same package) from home to holiday.

  • Verify customer
  • Go to manage viewing
  • Go to Holiday Home
  • Confirm general smartcard
  • Select general smartcard (From drop down list)
  • Select “from device” to “to device”
  • Confirm switch “on” date
  • Confirm switch “off” date (choose it)
  • Click on switch viewing
