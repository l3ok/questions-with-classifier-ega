Holiday Home device is a prepaid service that is flexible to the customer and can be reconnected upon customer's request when they go to their holiday home and it is disconnected when leaving the holiday home at any time during their billing cycle without affecting their home viewing and it is billed separately per day billing is done.

  • Log on to Clarity
  • Verify customer
  • Go to Manage viewing
  • Go to holiday home
  • Select holiday home smartcard
  • Select reconnect or disconnect option
  • Click on \off button
  • Then click reconnect or disconnect option
  • Go to manage viewing to check if the services are reconnected or disconnected
