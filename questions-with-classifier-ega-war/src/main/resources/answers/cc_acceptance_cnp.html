Your credit card is automatically registered for mail/telephone orders and Internet shopping. You need to activate your MasterCard SecureCode online before or during shopping on the internet. You must wait 24 hours after the card is issued before you can shop online.You can use your card for card not present (CNP) transactions for online shopping and mobile phone apps as well as mail order / telephone orders (MOTO) purchases. These transactions will be limited by the daily CNP limit you selected. For added security you can utilise MasterCard SecureCode once you have activated the service.CNP transactions are limit to your credit card account. If you want to use your savings account you will need to use your debit card.
