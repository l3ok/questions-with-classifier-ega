Your credit card can only be used for a specific period before it will expire. This is done to ensure that we can upgrade your card with the latest technology to ensure your protection against fraud and that it will remain readable (cards can eventually stop working due to many years of wear and tear damage). You will see that your card has an expiry date on the front. See (VALID THRU) This is the date that your card will stop working. Your card will expire at the end of the last day as indicated on the card.
