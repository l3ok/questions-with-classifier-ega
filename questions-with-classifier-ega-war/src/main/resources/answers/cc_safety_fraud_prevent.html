You can protect yourself from becoming a fraud victim by:

  • Never give your card and or PIN to anybody including family and friends
  • Ensure that fraudsters do not see you enter your PIN
  • Memorise your PIN and don?t write it down or keep it with your card
  • Make sure that you get your own card back after transacting
  • Set your daily card transaction limits to suit your normal spending habits ? do not set it too high. These limits can be changed when required
  • Registering for SMS update will notify you of all ?money out? transactions above R100
