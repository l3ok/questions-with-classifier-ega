If you are a new client, you will need to provide the following:

  • Proof of Residential Address (in your name, with your street address)
  • Identification document (Green bar coded ID, SA passport, SA ID card)
  • Latest monthly salary slip
  • Bank statement showing your last 3 consecutive salary deposits. If you are an existing Capitec Bank client, you will need to provide the following:
  • Latest monthly salary slip
  • Bank statement showing your last 3 consecutive salary deposits (if your monthly salary is not deposited into your Capitec Bank savings account). If you earn additional income other than a basic salary, you will need to provide your latest 3 consecutive salary slips for us to consider this additional income.
