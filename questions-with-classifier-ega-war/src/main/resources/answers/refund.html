A refund should only be processed when there's credit on the account and if customer pays cash make sure that relevant documents are provided (id copy, bank statement, proof of payment) also make sure you advise customer about refund turnaround time.

  • Log onto Clarity
  • Verify customer
  • Go to Manage account
  • Check if the customer has credit
  • If yes, click the refund button
  • Fill the amount to refund
  • Verify banking details
  • Choose reason for capturing
  • Add comment
  • Add documents (ID Copy, bank statement and proof of payment)
  • Click upload documents
  • Click refund
