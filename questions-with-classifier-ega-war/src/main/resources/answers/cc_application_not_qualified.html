Consider other Capitec credit offered and whether this meets your needs.If you only want a credit card or if you do not qualify for any other credit, ask the service consultant to provide you with the reasons why you did not qualify for a credit card.If you did not qualify based on affordability, attempt to settle some of your credit and then reapply.If you did not qualify because you did not meet our minimum requirements, remember that you are entitled to one free annual copy of your credit report from any credit bureau.
