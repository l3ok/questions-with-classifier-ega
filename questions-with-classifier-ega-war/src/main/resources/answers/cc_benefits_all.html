A credit card gives you access to a line of credit to use for planned or unplanned purchases, with the added flexibility of repaying it later. Paying with a credit card is easier, more convenient and safer than using cash. You can use your credit card globally wherever you see the MasterCard logo. If you use your card responsibly it may help you to build up a good credit record, which could lead to better offers for other credit applications.A Capitec Bank credit card has the following features:

  • Access to a line of credit to be used at your own convenience
  • Up to 55 days interest free
  • Manage your spending - you are in control
  • Global acceptance
  • Convenience and safety
  • Free basic travel insurance and lost card protection
  • Flexibility of a budget account
