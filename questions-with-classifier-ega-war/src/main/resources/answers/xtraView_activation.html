EXTRAVIEW IS activated when a customer would like to pay one subscription but have two / three different viewing environments. Customer needs two extraview compactable decoders to do so.

For an existing customer:

  • Verify the customer on Clarity
  • Go to â€œManage Viewing"
  • Go to Xtraview
  • (under device for Xtraview) click on create Xtraview (2decoders) or create Xtraview(3decoders)
  • Go to dropdown list under device for primary
  • Ask the customer to confirm the primary device
  • Go to dropdown list under device for secondary
  • Ask the customer to confirm the secondary device
  • Select activate
  • Go to manage viewing to check if xtraview is active

For new customers:

  • Add devices (then refer to step 1 from the above)
