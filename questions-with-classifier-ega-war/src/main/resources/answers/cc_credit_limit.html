Your credit limit is the maximum amount available to use on your credit card and is based on your credit profile and affordability.You can select a credit limit which is equal to or lower than what is offered.You can choose to use the full available credit limit or only some of the available credit limit.Straight and/or budget purchases can be made up to your credit limit. Straight: The full purchase amount or minimum percentage is payable onthe Repayment Date.Budget transactions allow clients to select a repayment period & the amount payable is based on the period.
